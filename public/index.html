About me

Welcome to my portfolio page! Here is a short overview of myself, as well of my work. My name is Simon, and I am a Web Developper in training. My journey started in 2022, this Year! Yet I am proud to say my advances have been very note worthy (Check out my Projects Yo). Programming is something that has found a way to my heart, the process of creating is all more exciting then just seeing it as a job. In my humble opinion, programming is progressively becoming something indespensible in today's technology advancing world. Machines were created to help humans in their tasks, automating what could be automated. And nowadays, the bond is stronger then ever!

What drives me forward?


A tick went off in my mind... It was a chilly winter afternoon as I was walking home from my School. I was deeply thinking about the book i've recently had read called "Homo Deus", publish by Yuval Noah Harrari. There, he talked about the future of technogology, OUR future living with these technologies. How are lives have been slowly shifting, and still are, to adapt to a connected world. How the machines we take for granted are becoming more and more advanced. How soon, these machines will be able to do so much more, totally replacing our traditionally functioning world. To become a better one, a much more technologically advanced one!


But good things don't come to you without a sweat, and nothing will change if people don't take the initiative. I decided I wanted to contribute to that future, a place full of mysteries and yet to be discovered greatness. The future is in our hands, I don't want to be just a sidewalk watcher, I want to make the changes myself. And for that, I code.
